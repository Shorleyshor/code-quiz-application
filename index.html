Code quiz App

  • You have 2 minutes to complete the quiz.
  • Timer starts after you answer the first question.
  • Every question you answer correctly gives you 10 seconds and for every wrong answers, 10 seconds will be deducted from the timer.
  • Your scores is measured by the amount of seconds you have left after game is complete.
  • The game is over after all questions are answered or when timer reaches 0.
